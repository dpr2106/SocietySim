# Society Simulator: Master Upgrade (Phase 3 & 4)

I have successfully completed Phase 3 and Phase 4! The simulator is now much more immersive, the AI is smarter, and the village truly feels alive.

## 🎭 Better Roleplay (Prompt Fix)
The AI has been given a strict **"SHOW, DON'T TELL"** rule. 
- They will no longer break the fourth wall by saying things like *"As a hacker..."* or *"I must prioritize my duties as a soldier."*
- Their dialogue is now heavily conversational, immersive, and speaks entirely in the first-person perspective as if they are truly living in the village. 

## 💬 Phase 3: Agent Conversations
Agents no longer just speak their thoughts into the void!
- As agents wander the village, if two idle agents happen to get close to each other (within 6 meters), they will stop walking and turn to face each other.
- The simulation will dynamically trigger a **new AI endpoint** that generates a short, 2-line back-and-forth conversation between those two specific archetypes. 
- You will see them both pop speech bubbles simultaneously to chat about life in the village!

## ⚔️ Phase 4: Factions & Rebellions
The society can now fracture if things get too chaotic.
- If the **Social Harmony** progress bar drops below **30%**, factions will form.
- The Anarchist, Villain, and Hacker will join the **"Chaos"** faction and spawn a glowing red ring under their feet.
- The Soldier, Politician, and Elder will join the **"Order"** faction and spawn a glowing blue ring under their feet.
- Once Social Harmony naturally recovers above 60% (thanks to the Elder and other peaceful agents), the factions will dissolve and the rings will disappear.

> [!TIP]
> Jump into the simulator and do a hard refresh (`Ctrl + Shift + R`). Trigger a massive crisis to watch the red and blue Faction Rings appear, and then click on agents to read their much-improved, immersive roleplay dialogue!
